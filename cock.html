Cock

Cock bird" generally refers to a male bird, especially a chicken, and is also known as a rooster or cockerel.

Scientific Name of Cock

The scientific name for cock is Gallus gallus domesticus.

Diet

Cocks are raised for both meat and eggs, with common feed sources including cereals, grains, and byproducts, along with protein sources like fish meal and soybean meal.

Habitat

In the context of domestic chickens, a male chicken, or "cock," lives in a chicken coop (or hen house), a structure designed to provide shelter and protection for chickens, along with an enclosed area called a "run" or "chicken yard" for them to roam.

Difference between hen and cock

Generally, roosters tend to be larger and more robust than hens. They have a more upright stance, with a larger and more prominent comb on top of their heads.
